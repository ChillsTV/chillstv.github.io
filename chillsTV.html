In this study, we are curious about Frisson.

Frisson is a psychophysiological response to rewarding auditory and/or visual stimuli that often induces a pleasurable or otherwise positively-valenced affective state. It is the internal, spreading shivers we sometimes experience when watching a video, listening to music, or in social situations.

Generally, they correspond to a sensation of feeling cold down your spine. Sometimes, frisson is accompanied by goosebumps. We use Chills and Frisson as synonyms.
